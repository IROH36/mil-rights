Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Mil-Rights is a free, offline reference guide. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This policy explains the minimal data the app stores on your device and how it is used.

Data stored on your device

The app stores two items in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere:

  • Saved topics (mil-favs) — a list of topic IDs you have bookmarked.
  • Profile settings (mil-profile) — your optional status, branch, and rank selections used to filter topics.

You can delete this data at any time by using "Clear Profile" in the app, removing saved topics, or clearing your browser or app data.

Data we do not collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking pixels
  • No device identifiers
  • No cookies
  • No accounts or sign-in

Third-party links

Each topic links to an official U.S. government website (e.g., uscis.gov, va.gov, dfas.mil). When you tap "Go to Official Page," you leave Mil-Rights and are subject to that site's own privacy policy. We have no control over and are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites.

Children

Mil-Rights does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, there is no special risk for children using the app.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub repository.
